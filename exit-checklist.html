Before you leave

Departure Checklist

Please complete each step before leaving. Tick items as you go. Standard checkout is 12:00 pm (noon).

Please send us a message when you leave. Thank you!

Bedrooms

  • Strip used sheets and place them in the laundry.
  • Make up the beds with the clean spare sheets provided (one complete set is in a pillowcase in each room).
  • Check drawers, wardrobes, and under the beds for personal belongings.

Bathrooms

  • Place used towels and face washers in the laundry.
  • Clean the bathrooms and leave them guest-ready (wipe sinks, clean toilet, wipe shower/bath).
  • Check bathroom cupboards and shower for personal items.

Kitchen & Dining

  • Wash and put away all dishes, pots, pans, and utensils. Run the dishwasher if needed.
  • Wipe down kitchen benches, stove top, and dining table.
  • Empty the fridge and freezer of any personal food. Leave pantry items you have not used.
  • Empty the kitchen rubbish bin and recycling basket under the sink.
  • Empty and wash clean the compost container.

Floors & Surfaces

  • Vacuum carpets and hard floors in all rooms used. Stick vacuum is under the stairs (wave hand inside door to activate sensor light).
  • Mop tiled floors.
  • Wipe and dust used surfaces.
  • Sweep outdoor areas (or use the leaf blower from the garage).

Outdoors & Pool

  • Check pool water level and add a chlorine tablet if needed.
  • Close and latch both pool fence gates.
  • Return outdoor furniture covers to any chairs where they were provided.
  • Return BBQ to its storage position. Turn off the gas bottle regulator.

Bicycles & Car

  • Lock and chain the electric bicycles in the garage. Pass the cable through each frame and lock it. Use U-locks if parked outside.
  • Return bike keys and car keys to the 3rd kitchen drawer.

Final Check

  • Check all rooms, cupboards, and drawers for personal belongings.
  • Check phone chargers, toiletries, medications, and valuables.
  • Turn off all air conditioning units, lights, and fans.
  • Close all windows.

Locking Up

  • Lock the laundry external door (deadlock).
  • Lock the glass sliding doors and screen doors.
  • Lock the internal door to the garage.
  • Lock the front door (deadlock).
  • Place the front door key back in the outdoor key lock box. The code is needed to close the box.

Notify Us

  • Send a message to Kayleen (+61 429 055 655) or Paul (+61 409 055 655) to let us know you have left.

Thank you for staying with us. We hope you had a wonderful time!
