Orientation Checklist

A quick walkthrough for your first hour. Tick each item as you go. Your progress is saved in this browser.

Keys & Access

  • Two sets of keys have been left for you — one on the kitchen bench, one in the laundry.
  • Key box is on the brick pillar near the front door. Ask us for the code. Instructions are inside the lid. Use this if you ever lock yourself out.
  • The laundry door is deadlocked for security — that is why keys are left on the bench.
  • Our rule: if a door is closed, treat it as locked or latched.

Security System

  • The JVA remote fob on the key ring operates the alarm. 1 chirp = armed, 2 chirps = disarmed. Please arm whenever you leave the house.
  • Indoor dome cameras (two) are only powered on when the alarm is armed. They are never on while you are home.
  • Two front cameras point at the street and are always on, recording on motion. One back veranda camera is off.

Safety

  • Fire extinguisher — on the laundry shelf near the kitchen door.
  • First aid kit — in the kitchen pantry (marked).

Known Issues

  • The shower door in the main bedroom ensuite is not fully secure — please be gentle with it. We apologise for this inconvenience.

Checkout

  • Confirm your checkout date and time with Paul or Kayleen. People have got this wrong in the past.
  • Standard checkout is 12:00 pm (noon) unless we have agreed otherwise.
  • You are expected to clean before you leave. See the Exit Checklist for details.

Good to Know

  • Contact details, Wi-Fi, and all house information are in the Guest Guide and the printed manual in the guest bedroom.
  • Please message us to let us know you have arrived safely.
